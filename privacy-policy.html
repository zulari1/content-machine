Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

This application is used to connect n8n workflows to Google services through OAuth 2.0. We collect only the information necessary to authenticate your Google account and let n8n access the APIs you explicitly authorize.

Information we collect

  • Your Google account email address.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens needed to maintain the connection.
  • Basic profile information returned by Google during sign-in, if permitted by the scope you approve.
  • Workflow-related data you choose to process inside n8n.

How we use information

  • To authenticate your account with Google.
  • To connect n8n workflows to the Google APIs you authorize.
  • To refresh access tokens so the integration keeps working.
  • To operate, debug, and improve the automation workflows you configure.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your Google data with third parties except when required to run the integration, comply with law, or use infrastructure providers that host the application.

Data storage

OAuth credentials and workflow data may be stored in your n8n instance and connected database, such as Supabase, to keep the integration working across sessions.

Security

We use standard operational safeguards, including environment variables and secret storage, to protect credentials. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your choices

You can revoke Google access at any time from your Google Account permissions page. You can also delete the credentials from n8n to stop future access.
